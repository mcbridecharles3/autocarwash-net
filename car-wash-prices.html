Frequently Asked Questions, Answered with Precision.

How often should I wash my car?

It depends on how much your car goes through! If you’re driving daily, especially on Wisconsin roads with salt, dust, and mystery grime, a weekly wash keeps your ride looking fresh and protected. If your car mostly chills in the garage, you can stretch it to every two weeks. For waxing, aim for every 2-3 months, and for deep interior cleaning, every 3-6 months—or sooner if your cupholders have seen one too many coffee spills! Want the easiest way to keep your car clean? Check out our Auto Car Wash membership and let us handle the dirty work! 🚗💦

What’s the best way to keep my car’s interior clean?

Step one: Stop eating tacos while driving. Step two: Get regular interior cleaning! Vacuuming crumbs, wiping down surfaces, and cleaning spills ASAP will keep things fresh. But for deep cleaning—especially for mystery stains and funky smells—our deep interior cleaning service will get your car looking (and smelling) like new again.

Will washing my car too often ruin the paint?

Nope! Washing your car regularly protects the paint, not ruins it—especially with a proper hand wash. Dirt, bird droppings, and road salt can eat away at your clear coat if left too long. As long as you’re using the right products (like we do), washing often is the best way to keep your paint looking fresh.

Does waxing really make a difference?

Absolutely! Waxing isn’t just about making your car look shiny—it’s like sunscreen for your paint. It protects against sun damage, water spots, road grime, and bird droppings. Plus, a good wax job makes water bead up and roll off like your car just got hit with a “nope” button. We recommend waxing every 2-3 months to keep your ride looking fresh!
