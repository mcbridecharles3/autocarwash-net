Your Ultimate Car Interior Cleaning FAQs Answered

How often should I get my car’s interior cleaned?

We recommend having your car interior cleaned at least every three to six months, depending on usage. For heavy use, more frequent cleaning may be necessary to maintain freshness and cleanliness.

Do you offer car interior cleaning services?

Yes, we specialize in car interior cleaning. From vacuuming and upholstery cleaning to odor removal and air freshening, we make your car’s interior spotless.

What’s included in your interior cleaning service?

Our interior cleaning service includes deep cleaning of carpets, upholstery, seats, and trim. We also clean the air vents, dashboard, and apply air fresheners for a complete interior makeover.

How long does a car interior cleaning take?

The time it takes depends on the size of the vehicle and the condition of the interior. On average, a thorough car interior cleaning takes about 1-2 hours.
